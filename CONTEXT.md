# CONTEXT

Domain language for this project. Every term here names a concept with a specific meaning in the system. Use these terms in code, documentation, and architecture discussions.

## Portfolio

The collection of professional artifacts displayed on the index route. Comprises three sections: experience, projects, and skills.

## Experience

A past or current job or role. Fields: company name, role title, period (date range), description, and optional external link.

## Project

A freelance, side, or open-source project. Fields: project name, role, period (optional date range), description, and optional external link.

## Skill

A named competency in a language, framework, or tool. Grouped into labeled categories (e.g., "languages", "frameworks", "tools").
